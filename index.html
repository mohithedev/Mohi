Migration SEO · Agency Client — US

WordPress → Webflow
Rankings Held. Speed 10×.

A 5-year-old WordPress agency site with 87 pages and 3,200+ blog posts. The client was losing ground to competitors with faster sites — and needed a complete rebuild without losing a single Google ranking.

The Challenge

Migrate 87 pages and 3,200+ posts to Webflow while preserving all SEO meta tags, internal links, and 301 redirect chains. Hard 8-day deadline — client had a product launch tied to the new site.

The Approach

  • Audited all existing meta tags and URL structures upfront
  • Built a staging Webflow project with CMS collections mirroring WP taxonomy
  • Migrated content in batches with a custom CSV pipeline
  • Configured 301 redirects before DNS transfer
  • Ran Lighthouse audits post-launch to verify scores

Creative Agency Portfolio
GSAP Animations, 9 Days.

A creative agency needed a portfolio site that reflected their brand — premium, animated, and fast. They'd been burned by a previous dev who delivered a sluggish site with broken mobile layouts.

The Challenge

7-page portfolio with GSAP scroll animations, CMS-powered case studies, and a horizontal scroll gallery — all fully responsive and loading under 2 seconds.

The Approach

  • Mapped every animation to a GSAP ScrollTrigger timeline before building
  • Used Lottie for the hero loader animation
  • CMS collections for case studies with multi-reference tags
  • Finsweet CMS Filter for the portfolio filter grid
  • Performance-tested on mobile at every stage — not just at the end
